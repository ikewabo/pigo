Our Story

Born From Nature,
Crafted With Purpose

PIGO Honeybees is a premium artisanal honey farm rooted in the rich, fertile landscapes of Nigeria. Founded on a belief that nature's most perfect food deserves to be protected in its purest form, we raise our colonies with patience, knowledge, and deep respect for the hive.

Our bees forage across undisturbed meadows and wildflower fields, producing honey of extraordinary depth — golden in colour, complex in flavour, alive with enzymes and natural goodness that no industrial process could replicate.

Every jar of PIGO honey carries the story of ten thousand flights, a thousand flowers, and one unwavering commitment: no preservatives, no added flavours, no added sugar. Just honey, exactly as nature intended.

Nutritional & Medical Benefits

What Honey
Does for You

Raw honey is one of humanity's oldest medicines. Modern science has confirmed what ancient healers always knew — this golden liquid is a nutritional and therapeutic powerhouse unlike anything else on earth.

01
🛡️

Immune System Fortification

Raw honey is dense with antioxidants — flavonoids, phenolic acids, and hydrogen peroxide — that combat free radicals and reduce oxidative stress. Regular consumption has been shown to strengthen immune response, reduce inflammation, and support the body's natural defence mechanisms against seasonal illness and chronic disease.

02
❤️

Cardiovascular Health

Studies indicate that raw honey may help lower LDL (bad) cholesterol while raising HDL (good) cholesterol. Its polyphenols have been linked to reduced risk of heart disease. Used as a substitute for refined sugar, honey supports healthier blood sugar regulation and may help reduce triglyceride levels over time.

03
🌙

Natural Energy & Sleep Aid

Honey provides a steady, sustained release of glucose to the liver, fuelling the brain through the night without sugar spikes. A spoonful before bed promotes the natural release of melatonin, supporting deeper and more restorative sleep cycles. Athletes use raw honey as a natural pre- and post-exercise energy source.

04
🌿

Digestive & Wound Healing

Raw honey contains prebiotics that nourish beneficial gut bacteria, improving digestion and reducing symptoms of IBS and gastric ulcers. Applied topically, its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties make it a clinically recognised wound healer — used in hospitals worldwide to treat burns, cuts, and skin infections.

The Land

Our Farm,
Our Hives,
Our Promise

Nestled along the Lake Coast of Ozalhodo in Challa, Manio Turf, the PIGO Honeybees farm sits on some of the most pristine and biodiverse land in Nigeria. Far from industrial agriculture and pesticide-heavy crops, our bees work in a clean, thriving ecosystem.

We practice ethical, sustainable beekeeping — never over-harvesting, always leaving the colony with sufficient reserves, and nurturing queen health through seasonal monitoring and natural colony management.

🌺
Wildflower Foraging Zones

Our bees roam freely across dedicated wildflower corridors planted and maintained year-round. Daisy, clover, acacia, and native bush flowers provide the rich and varied nectar diet that gives PIGO honey its complex, layered character.

🏡
Hand-Harvested, Cold-Extracted

Every comb is harvested by hand during the peak of each season. We use cold-extraction methods — no heat, no filtration — preserving the full spectrum of enzymes, pollens, and natural antibiotics that raw honey is prized for worldwide.

♻️
Regenerative & Ethical Practice

We are committed to sustainable beekeeping. No antibiotics, no artificial queen-rearing hormones, and zero pesticide use on or around our land. Our bees are partners, not production units — and we treat them accordingly.

📦
Premium Packaging, Zero Compromise

Each 75cl bottle is individually filled, sealed with a hand-stamped cork, and inspected before dispatch. Our black-and-gold packaging reflects the quality inside — built for the table, worthy of a gift.
